01 Orientation
Ungasan · Bukit Peninsula · South Bali

The southernmost point of Bali.
The highest point of the view.

Ungasan sits at the tip of the Bukit Peninsula — a limestone plateau that rises above the Indian Ocean like a natural balcony. A strategic position at the centre of the Bukit, between Uluwatu and Nusa Dua, away from the busy north — tranquil, exclusive, designed for those seeking privacy and natural beauty.

Not overcrowded. Not underdeveloped.
Right in between — and that's exactly the point.

02 The Vibe

Quiet luxury.
No pretence.

Ungasan offers a lifestyle that feels both elevated and grounded — attracting a mature, intentional crowd: families, wellness professionals, digital creatives. People seeking space, calm, and inspiration.

There's no heavy nightlife here. Just the gentle sounds of waves, birds, and ocean breezes. Yoga studios, spa retreats, and nature-based mornings are five minutes away in any direction.

This is where people come when they've already done Seminyak and Canggu — and want something that actually lasts. — The Honeycombers, on Ungasan's reinvention

06 Daily Life

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Daily living is made easy with nearby Pepito and Nirmala supermarkets, local produce markets, pharmacies, and household shops just a short drive away.

Ungasan is a hillside community with sea views — you can live near quiet lanes but reach beaches in minutes. The Bukit is car-dependent: a scooter or private driver is the standard. In return, roads are calm, air is clean, and mornings feel like mornings should.

  • Groceries

    Pepito · Nirmala · local produce markets within 5 min

  • Pharmacy

    Apotek Kimia Farma · Guardian · 24-hour care

  • Hospitals

    BIMC · Siloam · international standards in Nusa Dua

  • Wellness

    Yoga studios · spa retreats · nature on every corner

  • Schools

    International schools 15-25 min · multiple curricula

  • Connectivity

    Fast fibre · co-working spaces · reliable cellular

The guests who stay here don't want a hotel.
